# Day 3 – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Summary

## Objective

Performed exploratory data analysis on the Mutual Fund Analytics dataset using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly to identify investment trends, investor behavior, fund performance, and portfolio characteristics.

## Work Completed

### Data Visualization

Created more than 15 analytical charts covering:

1. Daily NAV Trend (2022–2026)
2. AUM Growth by Fund House
3. Monthly SIP Inflow Trend
4. Category-wise Inflow Heatmap
5. Investor Age Group Distribution
6. SIP Amount by Age Group
7. Gender Distribution
8. Investment Amount by State
9. T30 vs B30 Distribution
10. Industry Folio Growth
11. NAV Return Correlation Matrix
12. Sector Allocation Donut Chart
13. Top 10 Funds by 5-Year Return
14. Expense Ratio Distribution
15. Benchmark Index Trend
16. Morningstar Rating Distribution
17. Risk Grade Distribution

### Key Analysis Performed

* Analyzed NAV movement across all mutual fund schemes.
* Studied Assets Under Management (AUM) growth across fund houses.
* Evaluated monthly SIP growth trends.
* Compared category-wise inflows over time.
* Examined investor demographics based on age and gender.
* Analyzed investment distribution across states and city tiers.
* Measured industry folio growth.
* Computed correlation among mutual fund NAV returns.
* Summarized portfolio allocation by sector.
* Evaluated fund performance, expense ratios, ratings, and benchmark trends.

## Deliverables

* EDA_Analysis.ipynb
* 17 exported PNG charts
* 10+ documented EDA insights
* Organized chart folders for reporting

## Outcome

The exploratory analysis provides a comprehensive understanding of mutual fund performance, investor participation, market trends, and portfolio composition, forming the foundation for dashboard development in the next phase of the project.
